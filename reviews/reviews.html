Reviews

Aadu 2

Malayalam
(Comedy)

Cast : Jayasurya,Vijay Babu
Director : Mithun Manuel Thomas

Aadu 2 is a celebration,celebration for all those who missed out the first part in theatre's.The movie is a comic ride,which gives entertaintment to the core for all those who buy a ticket with their valuable money.Shaji Pappan and his boys creates a superb atmosphere throughout the movie,and goosebumps are guaranteed.Mithun Manuel Thomas has created a set of cult characters which have won the heart's of youth in Kerala.Yes,Shaji Pappan is an emotion.

Rating : 4/5

Verdict : Must Watch for Aadu fans,guys,dont miss this one.

Mayanadhi

Malayalam
(Romance,Drama)

Cast : Tovino Thomas,Aishwarya Lekshmi
Director : Aashiq Abu

A decently executed slow paced emotional love story.Those who like the touch of Ashique Abu movies will like this one for sure.Career best perfomance from Tovino Thomas.Aishwarya Lakshmi, who has played the female lead has also done pure justice to her role.

Rating : 3.5/5

Verdict : Those who wish to see a light,romantic movie can give it a try.

Masterpiece

Malayalam
(Action,Drama)

Cast : Mammootty,Poonam Bajwa,Santhosh Pandit
Director : Ajay Vasudev

Mass entertainer filled with cliche elements.Movie starts off with an average first half,better second half post interval and wrapped up with a decent climax.

Rating : 3/5

Verdict : One Time Watchable
